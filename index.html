ABOUT THE STORE

The Bean Warehouse opened in July 2016 with just one item: a video game-inspired keychain set. Now, we offer keychains, earrings, cosplay props, coasters, jewelry, and more, all influenced by video games, nature, and science. We specialize in acrylic because it's lightweight, durable, and versatile. Acrylic can be transparent to make elegant double-sided keychains, or even metallic or mirror for shiny effects. It can be glued to lapel pin or assembled into a figurine. There's boundless objects to dream up and make, and we hope to explore new ideas and push the boundaries of laser-cut acrylic crafts!
